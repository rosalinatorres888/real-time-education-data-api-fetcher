💰 Investment Efficiency Analysis

Understanding the relationship between education spending and outcomes

🔍 Key Investment Insights
  • Utah achieves the highest ROI (1.66x) with the lowest per-pupil spending ($9,552)
  • New York spends the most per pupil ($29,873) but ranks 15th in NAEP scores
  • Massachusetts balances high spending ($21,524) with top performance (NAEP: 288)
  • States with poverty rates above 20% show significantly lower NAEP scores
  • Every $1,000 increase in per-pupil spending correlates with only 0.8-point NAEP gain

🎯 Policy Recommendations

Evidence-based strategies for improving educational outcomes

📚 For Low-Performing States

  • • Increase per-pupil spending to at least $12,000
  • • Focus on reducing child poverty through integrated services
  • • Implement Massachusetts-style curriculum standards
  • • Invest in teacher training and retention programs

💡 For High-Spending States

  • • Audit spending efficiency and reduce administrative costs
  • • Implement performance-based funding models
  • • Focus resources on classroom instruction
  • • Study Utah's efficiency model for cost optimization

🚀 National Priorities

  • • Establish minimum funding floor of $10,000/pupil
  • • Create interstate education compacts for best practices
  • • Develop targeted interventions for high-poverty areas
  • • Incentivize states to adopt proven curriculum models
